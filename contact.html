We are recruiting Postdoctoral Fellows and PhD students.
The VAMOS group focuses on quantitative vegetation remote sensing, ecosystem modelling, radiative transfer, and multi-sensor observations to understand structural and functional ecosystem traits and their climate interactions.

Research Areas

We welcome applicants interested in broad topics including:
  • Monitoring ecosystem structural & functional traits
  • Ecosystem modelling (biochemical, hydrological, physiological processes)
  • Radiative transfer modelling & quantitative remote sensing
  • Solar-induced chlorophyll fluorescence (SIF), hyperspectral, and thermal observations
  • Forest 3D structure from multi-scale LiDAR
  • Understanding ecosystem–climate interactions via models and multi-source observations

How to Apply

Please send the following materials:
  • CV with publication list
  • PDF of representative work(s)
