Join Our Team

We are actively recruiting Postdoctoral Fellows and PhD students.

The VAMOS group focuses on quantitative vegetation remote sensing, ecosystem modelling, radiative transfer, and multi-sensor observations to understand structural and functional ecosystem traits and their climate interactions.

Why Join VAMOS?

We offer a dynamic research environment, access to state-of-the-art computational resources, and opportunities for international collaboration. We value curiosity and rigorous scientific inquiry.
